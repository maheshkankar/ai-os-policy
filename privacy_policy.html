Privacy Policy for AI-OS

Publisher: AccessBridge OS

Effective Date: December 5, 2025

Contact Email: support@ai-os.com

AI-OS ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application (the "App") collects, uses, stores, and safeguards your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of data to provide our business automation services:

A. Personal Information (Collected & Stored)

  • Account Info: Name, Email address (via Google Sign-In), and Phone number (via Firebase Auth).
  • Business Profile: Business name, address, and operational details you provide.

B. User Content (Collected, Processed & Stored)

  • Messages & Chat: Text commands and queries you send to the AI agent.
  • Media & Documents: Images or documents you upload for OCR (Optical Character Recognition) or analysis (e.g., invoice scanning).

C. Device & Telemetry Data (Collected & Stored)

  • Identifiers: IP address, Device ID, and Firebase Installation IDs.
  • Usage Data: App crash logs, performance metrics, and feature usage statistics.

2. Why We Collect This Data

Data Type Purpose
Account Info To authenticate your identity, manage your session, and secure your account.
User Content (Chat/Images) To process your business requests, perform OCR on documents, and generate AI responses.
Device Data To improve app stability, debug crashes, and prevent fraud.

3. Permissions and Sensitive Data

To provide its core features, AI-OS requires access to certain sensitive permissions. We request these only when necessary.

Accessibility Service API Disclosure

Why we use it: AI-OS uses the Android Accessibility Service API to function as an intelligent business assistant.

Specific Functionality: This permission allows the App to:

  • Read screen content to understand the context of your business tasks (e.g., reading an order detail on a third-party app).
  • Perform automated actions (clicks/scrolls) to streamline workflows you initiate.

Data Handling: Data collected via the Accessibility Service is processed locally or securely transmitted solely to execute the specific task. We do not use this API to mine sensitive user data (like passwords) for marketing.

Camera

Used to capture images of documents or products for AI analysis and OCR processing.

Contacts

Used to help you manage business relationships and integrate with CRM workflows.

4. Third-Party Data Processors

We share data with trusted third-party service providers to facilitate our services:

  • Google Firebase: Used for Authentication (Phone/Google), Analytics, and Crashlytics.
  • OpenAI / LLM Providers: Text and image content may be sent to AI providers to generate intelligent responses and analyze documents.
  • Google Cloud Platform: Used for hosting our backend infrastructure and databases.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Retention: We retain your personal information and content as long as your account is active to provide the service.

Deletion: You have the right to request the deletion of your data.

  • In-App: Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account to permanently remove your data.
  • By Email: Contact us at support@ai-os.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request".

6. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted using TLS/SSL (HTTPS).
  • Access Control: Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel and systems.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Services are intended for use by adults (18+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date".
