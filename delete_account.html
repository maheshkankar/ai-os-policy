AI-OS Account & Data Deletion Request

If you want to delete your AI-OS account and all associated data, you can request deletion using the method below.

How to request account deletion

Send an email to our support team at:

support@bridgeaios.com

Include the following details:

  • Your registered email address or phone number
  • Your full name
  • Subject: “AI-OS Account Deletion Request”

What data will be deleted?

  • Your account information (email/phone number)
  • Chat messages associated with your account
  • Uploaded images (OCR documents)
  • Activity logs associated with your account

What data may be retained?

  • System logs required for security and fraud prevention (up to 90 days)
  • Anonymized analytics data (cannot identify the user)

Processing time

Your request will be completed within 7–30 days, depending on verification requirements.

For any questions, contact: support@quantime.in
